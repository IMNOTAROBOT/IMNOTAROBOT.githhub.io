Music Projects

I have few music experiences when I was younger. During elementary school I used to go to some art appreciation classes where I did lots of dancing, painting, acting and music playing. Then during high school I studied guitar and music composition. I do not shine at playing the guitar. Rhythm was missing for me, but I was good with theory.

One day while my college friends where playing WoW I got my first experience with FL Studio. They loved my first sample and I got obsessed with producing music. I used it as a way of chilling, it helped me to relax from the fast life I got in the city.

Since 2015 I decided to spend more time in FL studio and gain more experience. Through my 3 compilations and the one I am working lately is so evident that I have been improving.

You can find some samples of my music projects in Soundcloud.IMNOTAROBOT
